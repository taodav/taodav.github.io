I'm currently a M.Sc. (Thesis) student at the University of Alberta. I recently graduated from McGill University with First-class Honours and on the Dean's Honour List in Computer Science. I was previously a research intern at both Mila and Microsoft Research.

My current research interests lie in model-building in a reinforcement learning context. Our recent paper on model-based exploration was just accepted as an oral presentation to NeurIPS 2020! You can check out our paper below under Publications. If you're interested in collaborating on something shoot me an email!
